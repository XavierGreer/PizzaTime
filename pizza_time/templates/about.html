About Us
We are Pizza Time Pizzeria and we are located at 123 Pizza Time Street in Downtown Colorado Springs, Colorado.
We make the most famous and favorited pizzas you will ever eat with some sides to go along with.
All of our ingredients are fresh.
Our Dough is made fresh in house every day.
We also offer a gluten free option of our pizza dough.
Our cheese is all natural whole milk from grass fed cows.
Prices are competitive, but we guarantee the quality of the pizza is worth it.
We also serve a large selection of local and domestic craft beers to go along with your pizza of choice.

We service Dine In | Take Out | Delivery

Here at Pizza Time Pizzeria, we offer delivery to a limited area.
Delivery hours are Monday-Saturday from 12pm-10pm each day.
Sorry but we are closed on Sundays.
Our delivery times average 45 minutes to an hour from a successful online order.
Our delivery cost is only a $2.00 additional charge.
